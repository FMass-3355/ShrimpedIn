Resume/CV

A resume or CV is a timeline of your career. It may be hard to get a job with no experience. However, it is still possible to build a career with no experience. The main thing that you want to focus on when creating your resume is to focus on the experiences that helped you develop skills that can translate into the job market. This does not have to be from a job or internship in it of itself, but rather something that you did in school, a personal project, or something similar. Your main focus when creating a resume is to make sure it relates to the job you are applying to or career field.

According to Indeed.com, one way to introduce your skills on your resume is by mentioning your education highlights and professional skills you have developed. You can check out more information and tips here.

You can also check out this article for a better step-by-step approach in creating your resume.

Don't forget to upload your resume/CV to ShrimpedIn!

Cover Letters and Their Importance

The best way to improve your chances of getting your dream career/job is by sending an exceptional cover letter. The big mistake job seekers make is not including one when they apply to jobs. The cover letter is an important element in your job application, it introduces to the recruiters who you are, why you are applying to the job, the value you bring to the company, and ultimately why they should hire you. If you want to get started, Indeed provides an in depth analysis on how to create your own unique cover letter.
